Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2026

SoftSplit d.o.o. ("we", "us", "our") built the Molitva / Prayer app ("the App") as a freemium application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The App works entirely offline. We do not require account creation, login, or any personal information to use the App.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Favorite prayers
  • Prayer journal entries
  • App preferences (font size, reminder settings)
  • Prayer streak data

This data remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time premium purchase and optional donations through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All transactions are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

The App may send local notifications (prayer reminders) if you enable them. These notifications are generated on your device and are not sent through any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
