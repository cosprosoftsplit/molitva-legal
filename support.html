Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings > tap "Restore Purchases". Your premium access will be restored if you previously purchased it with the same Apple ID.

How do I set a prayer reminder?

Go to Settings > Reminders > enable "Daily Reminder" and choose your preferred time. Premium users can set up to 5 reminders.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All prayers are stored on your device. No internet connection is needed to read prayers.

How do I change the text size?

While reading a prayer, use the font size buttons at the bottom right, or pinch to zoom on the prayer text.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your data (favorites, journal, preferences) stays on your device. We don't collect or transmit any personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
